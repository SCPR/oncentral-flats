New bike lanes in South LA: San Pedro St, Hoover St, Exposition Blvd and MLK Jr Blvd

May 17, 2011, 9:49 a.m.


By Kim Bui
OnCentral Staff
Comment

Tweet

The blog L.A. Eco Village has been reporting about new bike lanes all over South Los Angeles.

So far, they've documented lanes on San Pedro and Hoover Streets. Yesterday, they showed us a couple other new lanes west of Vernon Central — Exposition and Martin Luther King, Jr Boulevards.

Bike lanes are few and far between in South LA, but the city's five-year bike plan includes a number of new paths for Vernon Central, including one that will stretch for the length of Central Avenue from Second Street to Manchester. (See the whole map of bike paths here).

Seems plenty fitting that these bike lanes start popping up, since it is Bike Week, and CicLAvia is still in the midst of working to extend their route through South LA.
